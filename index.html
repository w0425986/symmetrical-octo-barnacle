Chickpea Tuna Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayo
  • 1 rib of celery
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1-2 sheets roasted seaweed or nori
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Drain and rinse the chickpeas
  2. Add everything to a food processor
  3. Pulse until incorporated in 10 second intervals, be careful not to blend it too much since we want to keep some texture
  4. Add to sandwiches or eat on crackers
